# Bharat-kart

#### Description: An innovative idea where you just need to enter the app name you want to download and it will show you only Indian apps related to your search and clicking on the app you need will direct you to the google play store download page. Such an innovative idea when our PM says to give a vocal to the local and also it is available on Google Play Store.

#### Tools Used: Android Studio, Firebase
